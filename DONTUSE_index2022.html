Feeling
Machines

In a time when phone apps define who our friends and lovers are, when AI have become artists, and algorithms decide the music that moves us we ask what it feels like to feel with machines. How do digital technologies intertwine with the most intimate aspects of our lives?

We are taking a deep dive into the sticky, messy, fluffy ways that we live our lives within the binary logics of digital machines.
