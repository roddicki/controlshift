Control Shift 2020

Bristol & online

2-18 October 2020

Control Shift 2020 reflected on the intimate relationships we had with technologies during the lockdown. The programme provided different lenses and approaches to explore how we connect digitally. Through workshops, discussions, installations and screenings we explored ways to reframe, demystify, and transform our relationships with digital technology.

Control Shift is an arts programme from Bristol, UK, bringing creative and critical approaches to technology.
