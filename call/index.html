Work with us!

We are looking for a production manager for the Feeling Machines Weekender, 30 March - 2nd April

Fee: £1,500 (approx 6 days @ £250 per day)

We need someone with experience in installing artwork, managing teams, and creating accessible, safe and engaging spaces for the following events:
- Algorave music night at Lost Horizon, 19:00-23:00 on Thurs 30 March (with set up on Thursday 30th in the day)
- Workshops and exhibition at St Annes House, 11:00-16:00 Sat 1 April (with set up on Friday 31st and de-install on Mon 3rd April)

Support needed will include:

  • risk assessments
  • sourcing and rigging screens and projectors
  • sourcing materials needed, including things like plinths / ways to hang prints etc.
  • liaising with venue staff, Control Shift artists and producers
  • supporting artists to ensure work is safely installed
  • managing production interns /volunteers to set up and install artists work on Friday 31st and de-install on Mon 3rd April
  • to support on the day of Sat 1st April

How to apply

If you are interested in this opportunity please send a CV and short covering email explaining your relevant experience and interest to Martha at control.shift.network@gmail.com

Please send your expressions of interest as soon as possible (and no later than 10 March 2023), as we will be recruiting on a rolling basis.

** If you feel you have other things to contribute to the network (beyond this call out) and would like to volunteer with us please email us or sign up for our newsletter for more volunteering opportunities.
